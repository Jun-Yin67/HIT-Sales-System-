# HIT Sales System

A database-driven sales and inventory management system developed for HIT Sdn Bhd. The system manages customers, products, orders, deliveries, vendors, and inventory records while supporting sales transactions and reporting functionalities.

## Features

* Customer management (Regular Customer, Agent, and Dropshipper)
* Product management for:

  * Sharifah Ready-to-Eat Products
  * Biodegradable Packaging Items
* Order and payment processing
* Delivery management
* Vendor and purchase management
* Inventory tracking and stock monitoring
* Sales and purchase reporting
* SQL-based information retrieval and analytics

## Database Modules

### Customer Management

* Store customer information
* Manage customer categories
* Track customer orders and transactions

### Product Management

* Maintain product records
* Categorize products by type and subcategory
* Monitor product stock levels

### Order Management

* Create and process customer orders
* Manage order details and discounts
* Record payment methods

### Delivery Management

* Track delivery information
* Support multiple delivery methods
* Calculate delivery fees

### Vendor & Purchase Management

* Maintain vendor information
* Record purchase transactions
* Track vendor payments

### Inventory Management

* Monitor stock availability
* Update inventory after sales and purchases
* Generate stock reports

## Technologies Used

* Oracle SQL
* Relational Database Design
* ERD Modelling
* Database Normalization (3NF)

## Sample Queries Implemented

* Generate Vendor Invoice
* Generate Customer Invoice
* Check Current Stock Levels
* Total Revenue by Customer
* Most Ordered Products
* Unpurchased Products Analysis

## Project Objectives

The system aims to:

* Improve sales transaction management
* Maintain accurate inventory records
* Streamline vendor and customer operations
* Provide useful business insights through SQL reporting
